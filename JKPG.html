What to do in Jönköping

Though for some Jönköping may seem like a small city, there are countless of things to do. Listed are only a mere selection of activities one can do, all of which I"ve done and guarantee are entertaining

  • Shopping at Asecs mall
  • Hiking at the many trails and parks
  • Take a day trip to Gränna by bus
  • Spend a day visiting each museum
  • complain about the sun seeting so early

Good places to go out

Being a University student, when asked for good places to go out I will instinctivley think on going out in the context of going out to clubs, hence below is listed the scedule of which clubs to go to on which days as a student.

  1. Monday - Shooter's: free games
  2. Tuesday - Kappa bar: free games
  3. Wednesday - Akademian: the student club
  4. Thursday - Bongo bar: karaoke night
  5. Friday to Sunday - many open clubs on the weekend but my personal preference is Bongo and Fabriken
